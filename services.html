Our Expert Services

Discover how I & F ASSETS LIMITED leverages its diverse expertise to deliver unparalleled value and accelerate your success.

Property Development & Estate Management

We specialize in the full lifecycle of property, from initial design and development to meticulous estate management. Our expertise covers transforming undeveloped land into thriving communities and managing diverse real estate portfolios for optimal value. We act as property agents, real estate agents, and manage the acquisition, disposal, sale, assignment, transfer, and dealing in real assets of every description, both in Nigeria and globally.

Our services extend to real estate maintenance, ensuring properties are preserved and enhanced, and we actively engage in the purchase, sale, letting, and disposal of both real and personal property.

Strategic Investment Services

As a dedicated investment company, we provide strategic guidance and opportunities across various asset classes. We acquire and hold debentures, debenture stock, bonds, notes, obligations, warrants, options, and other securities issued or guaranteed by companies, governments, or public bodies worldwide.

Our team meticulously invests and deals with moneys in a manner that maximizes returns, while ensuring compliance with all legal frameworks. We also manage, alter, dispose of, and deal with investments to adapt to market dynamics and client objectives.

Construction & Infrastructure Development

We offer comprehensive construction services covering every description of work. This includes meticulous planning, renovation, contract projection, liaison, supervision, erection, improvement, repair, alteration, fabrication, maintenance, and demolition of construction works.

Our expertise as building contractors extends to designing, constructing, renovating, drafting, planning, and projecting all kinds of construction projects, delivering high-quality infrastructure solutions from conception to completion.

Strategic Management & Procurement

We provide efficient and effective management and procurement services to streamline your operations. Our solutions are designed to optimize resource allocation, enhance supply chain efficiency, and ensure the timely and cost-effective sourcing of essential goods and services.

From operational oversight to strategic sourcing, we empower businesses to achieve greater efficiency and focus on their core competencies, fostering sustained growth.

Mineral Resources Development & Supply

Our involvement in the mineral resources sector spans sales, mining, and supply. We are active miners of precious stones, gypsum, gold, tin, and other valuable minerals.

Our operations include developing, rendering workable, excavating, digging, tunneling, quarrying, and working mines and mineral supplies. Furthermore, we supply and deal in (or manufacture) excavators, limestone, marble, precious stones, and a range of other mineral products, contributing significantly to the resource sector.

Property Investment & Sales

We specialize in profitable property investments and sales across various categories, including land, residential houses, and commercial properties. We actively acquire, hold, improve, alter, develop, let, sell, or otherwise dispose of land, buildings, and real estate of any description.

Our expertise extends to general dealings in landed property, property management, and acting as property developers, general contractors, dealers, renovators, and suppliers, ensuring maximum returns and seamless transactions for our clients.
